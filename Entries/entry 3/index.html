Summary


When a bunch of kids get lost on a family trip and discover a new world at the North Pole. Veronica is going on a cruise with her family and family friends. While roaming around, the kids lose their parents and decide to take the elevator back to their rooms. Little did they know, the elevator that they ride was a magical ride that accidentally transported them to the North Pole. Will they be able to get back and enjoy the rest of their vacation?
