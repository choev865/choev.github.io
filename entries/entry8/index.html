Soul Surfer
April 8, 2011

A natural talent in the sport of surfing, teenager Bethany Hamilton loses an arm in a shark attack. Bolstered by the love of her parents and refusing to give up, she plans to return to competition, although questions about her future continue to trouble her. Upon seeing the devastation in Thailand caused by the 2004 tsunami, Bethany discovers a greater purpose: to make a difference in the lives of others.

For this week, I decided to choose Soul Surfer because I thought the themes matched very well. In the movie, Bethany goes through a terrible shark accident that changes how she lives forever. She overcomes the obstacle of not having an arm by trying to live a normal life again and going to therapy. She soon goes back into the ocean and surfs again. My dream is not as tragic, but I also go through an accident that makes me scared to drive again. I am traumatized and scared to drive with my mom. However, I keep practicing and drive around the neighborhood, so that I can pass my driver's test.
