Summary


Juno
December 25, 2007

When precocious teen Juno MacGuff becomes pregnant, she chooses a failed rock star and his wife to adopt her unborn child. Complications occur when Mark, the prospective father, begins viewing Juno as more than just the mother of his future child, putting both his marriage and the adoption in jeopardy.


Dream
November 2022

I was back at church as a SAT teacher. I used to teach during the summer of 2021, but it had a lot of negative and postives. The whole SAT camp was very unorganized, but I loved all the students and teachers that I worked with. I found myself walking through the doors again and the hallway was empty. I pass by each classroom hoping to see a face I know. I couldn't belive that I was back and was about to teach another class again. I saw a lot of new faces and other teachers who were way older than I was. I started to get nervous because I felt unqualified. Then I realized, I was in charge of the 6th grade English class. I panicked because I was not experienced or ready at all. All the emotions I felt last year were happening again, but this time I was unprepared on the first day of camp. I didn't have a lesson or any sort of activity prepared, but I was heading towards my classroom. I peek in and I had a class full of students waiting for me. They didn't take me seriously beause I was so young and I got more nervous because I was unprepared.
