Summary


Harmony is a high school student at Whippany Park highschool. All she wants to do is be in a relationship, but she has one problem. She's a fairy living in the forest. She develops a crush on a boy in her marching band. Will she be able to fall in love like a normal high school student or will he find out her secret?
