Summary


Love, Rosie
May 5, 2015

Rosie and Alex have been best friends since childhood. As high school ends, she's starting to recognize that there may be feelings besides friendship between them. But she doesn't get the chance to find out for sure, because he heads to Boston to study at Harvard, while she stays put in England, where an unplanned pregnancy puts a halt on her own college plans. As the years pass and other romantic partners move in and out of both their lives, Rosie must decide whether Alex is her soulmate -- and, if so, what should she do about it.

Comparison

Show/Hide

For this week, I decided to re-watch Love, Rosie. I picked this movie because I was very late for my job interview and missed my opportunity to get hired. In the movie, Rosie and Alex keep missing their chances to be together. For example, Rosie read Alex's letter too late about his feelings. She rushed to tell him her feelings before his wedding, but arrived after the ceremony. This was the biggest missed opportunity throughout the movie, but in the end it worked out. I contrasted the movie and my dream because we were both late for very important events.
