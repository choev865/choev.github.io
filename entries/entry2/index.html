Summary


Midnight Runners
August 9, 2017

Ki-Joon and Hee-Yeol are new students at the Korean National Police University. They are not the best students, but they are sincere when it matters. They decide to go out one evening. Towards the end of their night, they witness a young woman being kidnapped. Ki-Joon and Hee-Yeol try to get the police involved, but they meet apathy and bureaucracy. The two police cadets decide to save the young woman's life before it's too late.


Dream
September 18 2022

I receive a report and I head upstairs to the apartment with my police officer partner. I was scared because this was my first real case and report. On our way up, I see a nose and knife sticking out of wall. I assumed that it was a victim buried in the wall and notified the other officers. The call from earlier led them to a brown door and a woman answered the door. I made small talk with her and she let us in her house. I didn't want her to know we were gonna arrest her so I kept looking around the house and complimenting how neat and decorated the place was. Then, as we walked towards the living room, the woman grabs a tissue and puts it in the candle. She shoves it into my face, but I grabbed her arm before it reached my face. The act was over. I tossed her to the ground and I duct taped her feet together. Before I could get to her hands, the woman grabbed her phone and started to text someone, "I swear someone's gonna kill me right now. I love you so much awekfwig..." I quickly grabbed her phone and continued duct taping her hands, while her partner prepared to take her downstairs. I told her, "Don't worry. You're not gonna die, you're going to jail."
