Summary
  When a scared police officer faces her fears and takes on her first real 
  case, things don't go on as planned.

  Veronica was a new police officer in a small quiet town that barely had any
   trouble, until a series of mysterious cases were reported. As she dives 
   into the cases with her team, she realizes they could be dealing with a 
   serial killer, known as nose killer. Will she be able to hunt down the 
   killer and solve her first case?

 way up, they see a nose and knife sticking out of wall. They assumed that it was a

 Veronica kept looking around the hosue as she complimented how neat and decorated the 
 area was. Then, as they walked towards the living room, the woman grabs a tissue and 
 puts it in the candle. She shoves it into Veronica's face, but Veronica grabbed her arm
