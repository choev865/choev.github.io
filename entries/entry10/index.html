Summary


Frenemies
January 13. 2012

It is a comedy about three pairs of friends whose relationships go from good to bad and back again. Several couple fall out over girls, boys and work. Can all these couples settle their differences and be friends again? One of the stories focuses on Avalon and Halley who created a web magazine called "Geekly Chic." They get invited to a publishing company in NY and offers to buy it. The company decides that whoever writes the best cover article will be the one and only senior editor that works for them, which turn them into frenemies.
