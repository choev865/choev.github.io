Summary


The largest tornado threatens to crush a small town, leaving kids and family members to find shelter and their loved ones. Veronica, an elementary school student, struggles to find her grandma's home as she runs from a huge tornado that is approaching her town. Will she find her grandma's home She must overcome the struggles and difficulties to survive the violent tornado. Will she survive and find her family?
